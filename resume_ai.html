FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does ResumeAI support?
ResumeAI supports PDF, DOCX, and DOC formats. For best results, we recommend uploading a PDF as it preserves formatting. Our OCR technology can also process image-based PDFs.
How accurate is the ATS score?
Our ATS scoring algorithm is trained on data from 500+ ATS systems including Workday, Greenhouse, and Lever. It has a 94% correlation with actual ATS outputs in our testing.
Is my resume data kept private?
Absolutely. Your resume data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256. We never share your data with employers or third parties. You can delete your data at any time.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel anytime with no cancellation fees. Your plan remains active until the end of the billing period.
Does ResumeAI support Arabic resumes?
Yes! ResumeAI fully supports English and Arabic resumes. Our AI models are trained on bilingual datasets and our interface supports both LTR and RTL layouts.
